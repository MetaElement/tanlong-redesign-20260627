# Tanlong Homepage Color Variants

## Aqua Clear

- File: `final/tanlong-aqua-clear-hero-1920x1080.png`
- Background: `backgrounds/aqua-clear-bg.png`
- Direction: clear aqua blue and emerald green, strongest environmental-technology balance.
- Use when the site should feel clean, credible, and close to modern environmental engineering.

## Morning Green

- File: `final/tanlong-morning-green-hero-1920x1080.png`
- Background: `backgrounds/morning-green-bg.png`
- Direction: sage green, mint, soft sunlight, warmer and more natural.
- Use when the site should feel more friendly, ecological, and less industrial.

## Silver Air

- File: `final/tanlong-silver-air-hero-1920x1080.png`
- Background: `backgrounds/silver-air-bg.png`
- Direction: silver white, pale blue, soft teal, more transparent and future-facing.
- Use when the site should lean toward advanced low-carbon technology and clean engineering.

## Production Notes

- All text, navigation, logo, metrics, CTA, and business entries are rendered by HTML/CSS.
- Generated backgrounds contain no intended text, logos, numbers, UI, or brand marks.
- Each PNG is exported at 1920x1080 and validated with Playwright for bad images, console errors, viewport fit, and header overlap.
